Purpose

I believe that the purpose of and most effective use of my personal web project is to help me once the bootcamp is complete. My personal website will be a first look for those interested in me and my work. Future employers will be able to have a glimpse into my life as a web developer; they will have first access to information about me, my resume, my portfolio of work, what I am interested in working in, what I excell at, and how I am willing to grow as a developer.

Audience

The intended audience of my personal website will be future employers, clients, customers, other developers, professional peers, future co-workers, people I network with, and my cohort.

Goal

My main goal of my personal website is to intrigue potentional employers in order to acquire a part time or full time job. Another goal is to attact freelance clients, provide a gallery of my work and what platforms I work best on, and get my name out in the community in a digital way.
